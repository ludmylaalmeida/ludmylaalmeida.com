Hack Alumni

Landing Page Design.

Project Goal.

I got invited to be a product designer in this project by the Hack.Diversity Alumni Association. Hack.Alumni is a collective of highly skilled Black, and Latinx individuals coming out of the Hack.Diversity program.

Their goal was to make the alumni association it's own organization and separate themselves from Hack.Diversity by create a branding identity and a website where alumni could find resources and stay connected in the community.

Duration

July 2020 - Present

My Design Role

Branding, Prototyping, UI design

Tools

Sketch, Illustrator, Zeroheight

Research.

Before diving into user research, I met with the team to learn more about the organization's mission and image. They were able to provide me crucial details and guided me to create a brand that is connected to Hack.Diversity since they as the alumni association were representing the organization.

I started the research by looking at other organizations with a similar missions to gather inspiration and domain knowledge. From my preliminary research, I found that there aren't many alumni association in tech that has created their own brand and aims to be independent from their original organization. For the ones that exist, most interactions are not intuitive and key features lack personalization. Based on those findings, I started brainstorming ideas for the branding.

Branding.

Since branding an organization goes beyond creating a logo, a colour palette and a collection of photography, I had to take into consideration the original identity from the Hack.Diversity brand. By looking at their brandbook I understood that we could integrate some of their established branding, so alums and companies could connect the two organizations. The logo concept is similar and uses a shape, it was also important for them to connect the two by using the "incubated by" business term. Our colour palette are colors that stand alone as its own brand but still match with the Hack.Diversity palette.

As part of the branding strategy we also did copywriting and defined the mission and pillars of the organization.

Mission

The 3 pillars to our organization: Community, Diversity, and Technology.

Pillars

Our mission is to encourage alumni to tap into this valuable community, stay connected, and support each individual to reach their maximum potential.
