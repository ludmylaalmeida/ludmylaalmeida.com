Liberty Mutual Insurance

Usability Testing.

Project Overview.

Usability testing was conducted with agents to test the new Liberty Mutual Quoting system that will eventually replace eCliq. A test report was created after the usability testing containing the final results.

Company

Liberty Mutual Insurance - uRad Team

Team

Ludmyla Almeida, Micheline Galindo, Stephanie Velez

My Design Role

UX Design

Tools

Powerpoint, Illustrator, Zoom

Methodology.

1. Sessions were done over Zoom

2. A questionare was created to test how well the user could perform each task.

3. One team member interview the agent/underwriter.

4. Other team members took notes over the user actions.

Task Completion Success Rate.

System Usability Scale (SUS).

The System Usability Scale is a 10-item questionnaire that measures the usability of the system. A SUS score above 68 would be considered above average and anything below 68 is below average.

Net Promoter Score .

The Net Promoter Score measures the willingness of customers to recommend a company's products or services to others.
